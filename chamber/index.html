News Release

Queretaro will become the main state for data science development in the country, as the state government announced the city has been chosen headquarters for the new Oracle Cloud region. The object of this project is to accelerate the expansion of the cloud services provider, taking advantage on the company’s flexibility and scalability. The benefits of hosting this center of services will reach more than just the economic aspect for all the industry and citizens of Queretaro.
