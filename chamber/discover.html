Discover Queretaro

Demographic Information

Queretaro is the 21st city in Mexico with the higher number in the amount of population, according to the 2020 census there are 2,368,467 living in the city.

With a territory of 11,690 km2, it is one of the smallest states in the country, however it is an important contributor to the national GDP.

Due to the state’s industry, low crime rate, and overall life standards, the population in Queretaro has increased in the last decade about 40% by constant migration into the state.
