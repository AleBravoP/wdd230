Recife Temple

The Recife Brazil Temple is the 101st operating temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Located in the city of Recife, capital of the Brazilian state of Pernambuco, the grounds feature mango and palm trees and an ornamental garden. This temple is larger than many LDS temples built during the same time in order to accommodate the 137,500 members of the church in northern Brazil.
